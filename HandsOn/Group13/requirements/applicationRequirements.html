Functional Requirements

The app will display the number and type of pets (dogs, cats, etc.) registered in the city of Madrid.

Users can filter the dataset by animal type, breed, and district/neighborhood of Madrid.

The app will provide simple statistics such as “Top 5 most common breeds” or “Number of pets by district.”

A map will show the distribution of pets across Madrid, with markers or heatmaps for neighborhoods.

May include vet location or recommendation (still studying this part).

Acceptance criteria: users should be able to understand in ≤15 seconds how many animals live in their area, what types they are, and compare with other areas of the city. Source and date of data must always be visible.

Optional: notifications or alerts can be sent when new census data is published or when there are specific campaigns (e.g., adoption fairs).

Non Functional Requirements

The app should be easy to use and mobile-friendly, with a responsive design.

Performance: statistics must load in less than 5 seconds for the introduced queries.

Scalability: the system should be able to support at least 250 concurrent users.

Security: personal data is never shown; only aggregated and anonymized statistics are displayed.

Availability: service should be online 24/7 with 99% uptime.
