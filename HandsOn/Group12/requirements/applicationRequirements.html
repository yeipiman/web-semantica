Proposed Application: GreenZones Madrid

Explore, filter and export the city's green areas using Linked Open Data.

General Description

GreenZones Madrid lets users browse municipal green areas (parks, gardens and other public green spaces), filter by district and category, and export selections to CSV and RDF (Turtle).

Objectives

  • Show a searchable list of green zones (by Distrito, Subepígrafe, and Destino).
  • Provide details for each record (Apartado, Nº de archivo, Naturaleza del inmueble, Situación Destino, Solar...).
  • Allow export to CSV/RDF following the gz: ontology.
  • Enable links to related datasets (tree inventory, land‑use, air quality nearby).

Functional Requirements

  • FR1: List + search + filters by district/category.
  • FR2: Detail page per green zone.
  • FR3: Export selected items to CSV and RDF (Turtle).
  • FR4: Basic analytics (count per district, category).

Non‑functional Requirements

  • NFR1: Responsive and accessible UI.
  • NFR2: Reproducible pipeline (OpenRefine → RDFLib).
  • NFR3: License and attribution shown in the app.
