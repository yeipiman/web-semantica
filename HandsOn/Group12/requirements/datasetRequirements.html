Dataset Requirements Evaluation

Dataset: Inventario de zonas verdes del Ayuntamiento de Madrid (2024)

Source: Portal de Datos Abiertos / Geoportal del Ayuntamiento de Madrid.
Local copy: Zonas_Verdes_2024.xlsx — convertible a CSV.

R1. Smart City domain

✓Yes — Urban & Environmental: municipal parks, gardens and green areas.

R2. Open format

✓Yes — Provided as Excel; can be exported to CSV and published as RDF.

R3. Open license

✓Yes — CC BY 4.0 (reuse allowed with attribution).

R4. Linkable with generic entities

✓Yes — Contains the district attribute; can be linked to administrative entities (e.g., GeoNames or a Madrid districts vocabulary). Future versions may include coordinates to link with WGS84/GeoSPARQL.

R5. Documentation

✓Yes — The municipal portal provides metadata and field descriptions.

R6. Multiple sources (optional)

!Partially — Single primary source; can be enriched with the tree inventory, land‑use maps, or air‑quality near parks.
