2. Criteria Evaluation

Dataset 1: Municipal Public Car Parks

  • R1. "Smart City" Domain: SATISFIED. Belongs to the Urban Mobility and Transport domain.
  • R2. Available as CSV file: SATISFIED.
  • R3. Open License: SATISFIED. CC0 1.0 Universal Public Domain Dedication
  • R4. Easily linkable: SATISFIED. Includes LATITUDE and LONGITUDE.

Dataset 2: Traffic. Average intensity on working days

  • R1. "Smart City" Domain: SATISFIED. Belongs to the Mobility and Traffic Management domain.
  • R2. Available as CSV file: SATISFIED.
  • R3. Open License: SATISFIED. CC0 1.0 Universal Public Domain Dedication
  • R4. Easily linkable: SATISFIED. Although this dataset does not contain coordinates directly, the "measurement points" can be geographically linked through other datasets on the portal that do contain their exact location (such as the "List of permanent traffic intensity measurement stations").

Dataset 3: BiciMAD Stations

  • R1. "Smart City" Domain: SATISFIED. It is part of the Sustainable Mobility area.
  • R2. Available as CSV file: SATISFIED. Static dumps exist.
  • R3. Open License: SATISFIED. CC0 1.0 Universal Public Domain Dedication
  • R4. Easily linkable: SATISFIED. Each station is defined by its LATITUDE and LONGITUDE.
