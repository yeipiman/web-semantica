Group 08 – Analysis and Resource Naming Strategy

1. Dataset Analysis

Selected datasets

  • Air Quality in Barcelona – measurements of pollutants (NO₂, PM₁₀, etc.) from city monitoring stations.
  • Traffic Street Sections with Weather Forecast – information about traffic intensity, occupation, and weather conditions with a 15-minute prediction.
  • Bike Parking Service in Barcelona – locations, types, and capacities of public bike parking points.

Data structure and types

DatasetMain columnsNotes
Air quality station_id, station_name, pollutant, value, unit, timestamp, district Numeric values for pollutant concentration; time-series data.
Traffic + weather section_id, street, district, intensity, occupation, speed, weather, forecast, temperature, humidity Combines mobility and meteorological values, updated frequently.
Bike parking id, address, district, lat, lon, type, capacity Static dataset with spatial coordinates.

Value ranges

  • Pollution values usually between 0–200 µg/m³.
  • Traffic intensity 0–100, occupation 0–1, average speed 0–60 km/h.
  • Temperature between 0–35 °C, humidity 0–100 %.

3. Resource Naming Strategy

The following URI structure will be used for both the ontology and the generated RDF data:

TypeBase URI patternExample
Ontology http://group08.linkeddata.es/ontology/BCNData# http://group08.linkeddata.es/ontology/BCNData#Station
Resources http://group08.linkeddata.es/resource/{class}/{identifier} http://group08.linkeddata.es/resource/station/08019004
  • Domain: group08.linkeddata.es
  • Ontology pattern: hash URIs (#)
  • Data pattern: slash URIs (/)
  • Content negotiation: .html for human view, .ttl for RDF data.

4. Summary

The datasets are well structured, open, and suitable for Linked Data publication. The defined URI strategy ensures unique, persistent identifiers for classes and resources, allowing future linking with external sources such as DBpedia or Wikidata.
