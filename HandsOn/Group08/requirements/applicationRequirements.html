Application Requirements - Group 08

Application Name

BCN Mobility & Air Explorer

Goal

The goal of the application is to visualize and compare different smart city data from Barcelona, mainly air quality, traffic, and bicycle parking information.

Main Features

  • Show air quality data by station on a simple map.
  • Display traffic sections.
  • Show bike parking locations and relevant contact information.
  • Allow filtering by district or pollutant type.

Users

The application is mainly for students and citizens who want to explore city data in a simple way.

Interface Sketch

The application will have one main map page with layers for air, traffic, and bikes. Each layer can be turned on or off by the user.
