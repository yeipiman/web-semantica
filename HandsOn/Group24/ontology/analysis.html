Hands-On 2 — Data & Licensing Analysis

This document analyses three CSV datasets provided by the group, focusing on (1) the data structure and value ranges, and (2) the licensing of the data source(s) and the proposed license for the generated Linked Data.

Datasets in scope

  1. Urban Tree Inventory — Green Areas, Districts of Madrid
    File: arbolado-zonas-verdes-madrid.csv
  2. Environmental protection expenditure by enterprises — Total industry (Spain)
    File: gasto-de-las-empresas-en-proteccion-ambiental-total-industria.csv
  3. Average atmospheric pollution — Nitrogen Dioxide (NO₂)
    File: valor-medio-de-contaminacion-atmosferica-dioxido-de-nitrogeno-no2.csv
